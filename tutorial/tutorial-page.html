Welcome to Feedback!

Thanks for taking the extension for a test drive! We’re here to help you track, analyze, and engage with your news. The plugin has two main features:

The Feedback Window and the News Dashboard.

Feedback News Team

Try it now!

To get you started, we flagged this page as an article. Click on the Chrome Extension to get a feel for the Window.

Write some notes, add some tags, and make sure to leave a rating!

Checking out your dashboard is easy - press the button and get taken to an analytics pages based on your reading habits. Enjoy!

Feedback Window

You can rate and evaluate your news, as well as see how the rest of the feedback community views the article you’re reading.

You can tag the article and leave yourself a note about what you’ve read.

News Dashboard

You can see your reading habits visualized in chart form.

You can organize your news and search it by author, publisher, date, or keyword.
