Welcome to Feedback!

Thanks for taking the extension for a test drive!

We’re here to help you track, analyze, and engage with your news.

The extension consists of two main features:

The Feedback Window and the News Dashboard.

Feedback Window

You can rate and evaluate your news, as well as see how the rest of the feedback community views the article you’re reading.

You can tag the article and leave yourself a note about what you’ve read.

News Dashboard

You can see your reading habits visualized in chart form.

You can organize your news and search it by author, publisher, date, or keyword.
